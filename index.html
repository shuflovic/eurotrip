Welcome, Adventurers!

Hey Peter and Susie! This is your one-stop funky guide to your 3-day stints in each city. Day 1: Arrive and chill. Days 2 & 3: Explore like pros. Day 4: Hop to the next spot. Focus on those twinkly Christmas markets – they're the real stars! 🎄

Pro tip: If you get lost, just follow the smell of hot wine. It never lies! 🍷

Prague: The City of a Hundred Spires (and Tasty Bites)

Food to Try:

  • Utopenec (pickled sausage – sounds weird, tastes awesome!)
  • Vepřo-knedlo-zelo (pork, dumplings, cabbage – classic Czech comfort)
  • Nakládaný hermelín (marinated cheese – cheesy heaven)

Drinks to Sip:

  • Becherovka (herbal liqueur – warms you up!)
  • Hot wine (perfect for chilly evenings)

Places to Visit:

Cafes, restaurants, historic buildings – wander the streets! (Add your faves here later)

Christmas Market Focus:

Head to Old Town Square for the massive tree, stalls with ornaments, and grilled sausages. Magical vibes all around! 🎅

Watch out for the astronomical clock – it might steal your time! ⏰

Bratislava: Slovakia's Hidden Gem

Food to Try:

  • Bryndzové halušky (potato dumplings with sheep cheese – national treasure!)

Drinks to Sip:

  • Zlatý Bažant beer (golden pheasant – crisp and local)
  • Hot wine (keeps the holiday spirit flowing)

Places to Visit:

  • Zlatý Bažant (brewery or pub? Cheers!)zlaty bazant
  • Lačinka (add text later – maybe a cozy spot?)
  • Suvlaki (Greek twist in Slovakia? bistrosuvlaki.sk
  • Slovak National Radio Building (architectural oddity – looks like an upside-down pyramid!)

Christmas Market Focus:

Main Square market: Wooden huts, handmade crafts, lokše pancakes, and mulled wine. Cozy and less crowded than others! ❄️

If the radio building talks back, you've had too much Becherovka! 📻

Budapest: The Pearl of the Danube

Food to Try:

Local specialties – maybe goulash or chimney cake? (Add more later!)

Drinks to Sip:

  • Hot wine (universal holiday warmer)
  • Local beers or pálinka if you're brave!

Places to Visit:

  • Szimpla (ruin bar – quirky and fun!)
  • Michael Jackson Tree (a tree dedicated to MJ? Dance around it!)

Christmas Market Focus:

Vörösmarty Square: Giant advent wreath, artisan gifts, kürtőskalács, and lights everywhere. Don't miss the basilica market too! 🌟

If you see MJ moonwalking by the tree, it's time for bed! 🌳🕺

Tips & Advice: Don't Get Lost in Translation!

  • Wear comfy shoes – cobblestones are sneaky.
  • Learn "thank you": Děkuji (Prague), Ďakujem (Bratislava), Köszönöm (Budapest).
  • Christmas markets = crowds + pickpockets – keep wallets close!
  • Hydrate with water, not just hot wine. 😉
